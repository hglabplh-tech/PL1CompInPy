# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in PL1CompInPy a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreement.
- Give feedback about ideas and code, not about people.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and work to correct them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sustained disruption of project discussions or development work.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent exclusion from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the project maintainers. Reports will be reviewed with care and handled as privately as practical.
